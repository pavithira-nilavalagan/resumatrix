Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about building your professional resume with our platform.

Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely! All your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We never store, transmit, or have access to your personal information. Your resume data stays on your device only.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is required to build your resume. You can start creating immediately. However, creating a free account allows you to save multiple resumes and access your history across devices.
What templates are available?
We offer 6 professionally designed templates: Classic, Minimal, Compact, Overleaf Modern, Modern Developer, and Pro Resume. Each template is optimized for ATS systems and can be customized with your preferred accent color.
Can I export my resume as PDF?
Yes! You can download your resume as a print-ready PDF using the Download button. The PDF is formatted exactly as you see in the preview, maintaining all styling, colors, and layout.
Is my resume ATS-friendly?
All our templates are designed with ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) in mind. They use clean, parseable layouts with proper semantic structure. You can also use the built-in ATS scanner to check your resume's compatibility.
Can I share my resume with others?
Yes! You can toggle your resume to Public mode and generate a shareable link. This allows you to send your resume to recruiters or colleagues without requiring them to download a PDF file.

Still have questions? Contact our support team and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
